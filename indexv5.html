HVHS Badminton

Rules

  • You play 4 games of singles and 2 games of doubles
  • Games are played to 21, switching sides when someone reaches 11 points, and for premier teams, games are played to 31, switching sides when someone reaches 16 points
  • When you have an even number of points you serve on the right side, and when you have an odd amount of points you serve on the left side (Refer to the image below)
  • Serves must be underarm
